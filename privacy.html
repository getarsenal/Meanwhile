Privacy Policy — Meanwhile

Last updated: 15 June 2026

Meanwhile is a personal job-interview tracking app. We designed it to keep your data in your hands. This policy explains what the app stores and where it goes.

What the app stores

Meanwhile lets you record information about your job search: companies, roles, interview rounds, the people you meet, your notes, compensation details, job descriptions, and an optional copy of your résumé. You enter all of this yourself.

Where your data lives

  • On your device. By default, everything is stored locally on the device using your browser/app local storage. It never leaves your device unless you turn on Cloud Sync.
  • Optional Cloud Sync. If you choose to enable sync, your data is sent to a database that you create and control (a Supabase project you set up with your own account and keys). Meanwhile has no servers of its own and never receives, stores, or sees your data. Sync traffic goes directly from your device to your database.

What we collect

Nothing. Meanwhile contains no analytics, no advertising, no trackers, and no third-party SDKs that collect personal information. The developer does not have access to your data.

Third-party services

These are used only when you choose a feature that needs them, and only with the specific content that feature requires. Meanwhile has no servers of its own.

  • AI features (optional). If you turn on an AI engine, the text you ask it to handle — for example a pasted job description, an interview brief, or a résumé/PDF you upload — is sent to the provider you select: Google Gemini (default), Groq, OpenAI (ChatGPT), Anthropic (Claude), or your own Supabase proxy. Their respective privacy policies apply. No AI service is contacted unless you enable one; the copy-paste option sends nothing automatically.
  • Company logos & briefs (optional). To show a company's logo, the app may look up a company name you entered via a public logo/autocomplete service and request a favicon. If you paste a job-posting URL, that URL is sent to a public reader service to return the page text.
  • Cloud Sync (optional). Only if you enable it, and only to the Supabase project you created and control.

Deleting your data

You can edit or delete any item at any time inside the app, or export a backup file. If you use Cloud Sync, deleting the Supabase project removes the cloud copy. Removing the app removes the local copy.

Children

Meanwhile is intended for adults managing their own job search and is not directed at children.
